How It Works

From first contact to full transition

Our structured process ensures every network member gets the right support at every stage of their electric aviation journey.

1

Initial Assessment

We evaluate your airport or organisation's current infrastructure, energy capacity, and strategic goals to understand your starting point.

2

Tailored Roadmap

NRG2fly develops a customised transition roadmap — including infrastructure investments, regulatory steps, and partnership introductions.

3

Network Integration

You're connected with the right partners in our network: technology providers, airlines, and other airports on similar journeys.

4

Ongoing Support

As the electric aviation landscape evolves, NRG2fly continues to support your organisation with updated guidance, new partnerships, and funding opportunities.

Live Network

Chargers active across the Netherlands & beyond

Our airports are already live on the SkyZero platform, making NRG2fly charging points visible and bookable for pilots across Europe.

Our Roadmap

Expanding across Europe in phases

We are rolling out charging infrastructure in high-growth markets in three phases. Starting in the Netherlands and neighbouring countries, the network will expand to cover all major regional aviation hubs by 2032.

Digital Platform

Smart energy management for airports

Our CPO/MSP platform gives airports and pilots real-time visibility into charging availability, energy consumption, and grid load — from any device.

Pilots can check charger status before departure. Airport managers get full control over energy scheduling and billing. NRG2fly handles the infrastructure complexity so you can focus on flying.
