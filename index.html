Our Mission

We help airports prepare for electric traffic and become energy hubs.

The transition to electric aviation is already underway. NRG2fly bridges the gap between ambitious climate targets and practical airport infrastructure — ensuring regional airports are ready to serve the next generation of electric aircraft.

We work with airports, energy providers, regulators, and airlines to design and implement the charging infrastructure, grid connections, and operational frameworks that will power tomorrow's flights.

What We Do

Building the backbone of electric aviation

Infrastructure Assessment

We audit existing airport infrastructure and identify the steps needed to support electric aircraft charging at scale.

Energy Hub Development

We help airports negotiate grid connections, renewable energy contracts, and battery storage solutions to become self-sufficient energy hubs.

Regulatory & Operational Support

Navigating the complex regulatory landscape of electric aviation with guidance from our experienced network of aviation and energy experts.

Network & Partnerships

Connecting airports with electric aircraft manufacturers, airlines, and energy providers to build a thriving regional electric aviation ecosystem.
