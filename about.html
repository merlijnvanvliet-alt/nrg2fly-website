Our People

The team behind NRG2fly

A multidisciplinary group of aviation, energy, and technology experts united by one mission: making electric aviation a reality across Europe.

Management Team

Leadership
Jacco Bink

Consulting Director

With a background at KLM and Alliander, Jacco brings deep expertise in aviation and energy systems. As Consulting Director at NRG2fly, he leads the rollout of charging infrastructure at airports across the Netherlands and Europe.

Partnerships Director

Co-owner of Europe's first electric flight academy and board member of the Electric Flying Connection, Merlijn combines a background in brand strategy with a passion for electric aviation. He leads NRG2fly's European partnerships and ecosystem building.

Founders

Where it all started
Jurjen de Jong

EV-charging Entrepreneur

In 2011 Jurjen founded GreenFlux, one of the early pioneers in eMobility, growing it into a globally recognised platform with over 150 million charging sessions before its acquisition in 2021. Spotting the same opportunity in electric aviation, he co-founded NRG2fly in 2022 and chairs the Electric Flying Connection.

✈️ Pilot in training
GreenFlux Accenture
Maarten Steinbuch

Scientist & E-mobility Expert

Maarten is Distinguished Professor at TU/e and Scientific Director of Eindhoven Engine. As a serial tech entrepreneur and keynote speaker he is a leading voice at the intersection of science, mobility and sustainability.

TU/e FD Eindhoven Engine
Jeroen Kroonen

Sustainable Energy Expert

Jeroen is a pioneer in sustainable mobility with deep expertise in airport energy systems, from grid connections to storage and solar. As co-founder of NRG2fly he connects his regional roots in Brabant with NRG2fly's European ambitions.
